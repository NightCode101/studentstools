## NEXT: Deploy to Vercel (3 Minutes)

1. Go to **https://vercel.com**
2. Click "Sign Up → GitHub"
3. Authorize GitHub
4. Click "Add New Project"
5. Select "studentstools" repo
6. Click "Deploy"
7. **Wait 5 minutes**
8. Your site is live at `studentstools.vercel.app` ✅

---

## THEN: Buy Domain (2 Minutes)

1. Go to **https://namecheap.com** (or GoDaddy)
2. Search: `studentstools.site`
3. Buy it (**$2** 🎉)
4. Note your registrar login

---

## FINALLY: Connect Domain (5 Minutes)

1. In **Vercel Dashboard** → Settings → Domains
2. Add: `studentstools.site`
3. Copy Vercel nameservers
4. Go to **Namecheap/GoDaddy**
5. Update DNS → Replace with Vercel nameservers
6. Save
7. **Wait 24-48 hours** ⏰
